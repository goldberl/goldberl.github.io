About Me

Hello! My name is Leah Goldberg. I graduated from Dickinson College in May 2023 with a B.S. in Computer Science and Japanese. I found my love for programming through an introductory computer science course in my sophmore year of college. Ever since, I have enjoyed the learning many technical skills and the creative and logical process of programming.

When I was young, I studied several foreign languages including Chinese, Spanish, and German. I have always loved learning about other cultures and diversifying my understanding of this world. In college, I started studying Japanese. After I graduated, I attended an Intensive Japanese programming at Nanzan University for 4 months while living with a wonderful host family in Nagoya.
